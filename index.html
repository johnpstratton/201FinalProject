Welcome to MineHound! Are you ready to experience the most exciting new puzzle/memory game on the web?! Look no further because MineHound offers an interactive experience for all users. Simply enter your name and use your arrow keys on your keyboard to navigate the red block to the trophy(gold block). Look out though, there might be some MINES in your way. If you win checkout out your score on our leaderboard.
